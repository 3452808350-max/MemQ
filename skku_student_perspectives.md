# SKKU STUDENT LIFE - REAL PERSPECTIVES

## 🎥 WHAT STUDENTS ACTUALLY SAY:

### **Academic Life:**
- **"Intense but rewarding"** - Korean universities are known for rigorous academics
- **"Group projects are common"** - Collaborative work is emphasized
- **"Professors are accessible"** - Office hours and mentorship available
- **"English-taught courses vary"** - Check specific department offerings

### **Social Life:**
- **"Clubs are everything"** - Joining a 동아리 (club) is key to social life
- **"International student community is tight-knit"** - You'll make friends quickly with other internationals
- **"Korean students are friendly but busy"** - They have intense schedules too
- **"Drinking culture exists"** - Social drinking is common but optional

### **Daily Life:**
- **"Cafeterias are cheap and good"** - Campus food is affordable
- **"Public transport is excellent"** - Seoul's subway system is world-class
- **"Four distinct seasons"** - Hot humid summers, cold dry winters
- **"Everything is digital"** - From payments to school administration

## 🏠 HOUSING REALITIES:

### **On-Campus Dorms:**
- **Pros:** Convenient, cheaper, social opportunities
- **Cons:** Rules (curfews, visitors), shared facilities, limited space
- **Cost:** ~$300-500/month including meals

### **Off-Campus Options:**
- **Goshiwon:** Tiny studio rooms, very cheap (~$200-300/month)
- **One-room:** Small apartment with kitchen/bath (~$400-700/month)
- **Share house:** Room in shared apartment (~$300-500/month)

### **International Student Housing:**
- Special dormitories for international students
- More English support
- Cultural exchange programs built-in

## 💰 COST OF LIVING ESTIMATE:

### **Monthly Expenses (approx):**
- Housing: $300-600
- Food: $200-400  
- Transportation: $50-100
- Phone/Internet: $50-100
- Miscellaneous: $100-200
- **TOTAL:** $700-1400/month

### **Tips to Save Money:**
- Use campus facilities (library, gym, etc.)
- Eat at school cafeterias
- Use public transportation
- Shop at local markets vs convenience stores
- Look for student discounts everywhere
