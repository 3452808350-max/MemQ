## 📊 动态图（有时间变化）

### 3种分段思路

1. **整体+特殊**：整体上升/下降，某个阶段例外
2. **整体+显著**：整体趋势 + 某个阶段明显变化
3. **分阶段**：起点终点一致，分段描述

### 趋势词

| 上升 | 下降 | 平稳 |
|------|------|------|
| increase, rise, grow | fall, drop, decline | remain stable |
| surge, soar | plummet, plunge | plateau |

### 极值
- reach a peak at 90%
- reach a low point at 20%
- bottom out at

### 程度
- 强: significant, substantial, dramatic
- 中: moderate, steady, gradual  
- 弱: slight, slow

## 🔄 静态图（无时间变化）

### 对比（差距大）

| 句型 | 意思 |
|------|------|
| A is significantly higher than B | A比B高很多 |
| A is almost twice as many as B | A是B的2倍 |
| significant disparity/gap | 差距悬殊 |
| A, followed by B | A最高→B |

### 相似（差距小）

| 句型 | 意思 |
|------|------|
| not a great deal of difference | 差距不大 |
| similar to | 与...相似 |
| almost the same | 几乎相同 |
| comparable | 可比 |
