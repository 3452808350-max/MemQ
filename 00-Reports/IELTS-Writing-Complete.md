# 📊 PART 1: Task 1 动态图

## 一、图表类型

| 类型 | 英文 | 特征 |
|------|------|------|
| 动态图 | Dynamic chart | 有时间变化，看趋势 |
| 静态图 | Static chart | 无时间变化，只比大小 |

**动态图类型：** Line chart, Bar chart (带时间), Table (带时间)

---

## 二、三种分段思路

### 思路1：整体 + 特殊
整体上升/下降，除了某个阶段有变化

> Focusing on national and international fixed line, the time spent on it increased steadily from about 37 billion minutes in 1995 to just over 60 billion minutes in 2002 **although the figure remained almost the same between 2001 and 2002**.

### 思路2：整体 + 显著变化
整体趋势 + 某个阶段特别明显的变化

> Turning to mobile calls, there was a significant rise from nearly 3 billion minutes in 1995 to approximately 43 billion minutes in 2002. **This growth was particularly noticeable from 1999 to 2002**, during which there was an increase of about 30 billion minutes.

### 思路3：分阶段描写
起点终点一致，只能分阶段描述

> When it comes to local fixed line, the figure experienced a moderate growth from just over 70 billion minutes in 1995 to just under 90 billion minutes in 1998. **After reaching a peak at 90 billion minutes in 1999**, the total number dropped steadily to the original level in 2002.

# 📊 PART 2: Task 1 静态图

## 一、对比句型（差距大）

| 句型 | 例子 |
|------|------|
| **A is significantly higher than B** | A (66%) is significantly higher than B (34%) |
| **A is almost twice as many as B** | Almost twice as many in A as in B |
| **A significantly higher percentage** | A significantly higher percentage of people... |
| **significant disparity** | There is a significant disparity between A and B |
| **followed by** | A is highest, followed by B |

## 二、相似句型（差距小）

| 句型 | 例子 |
|------|------|
| **not a great deal of difference** | There is not a great deal of difference between... |
| **similar to** | The percentage of A is similar to B |
| **almost the same** | The number of A and B is almost the same |
| **comparable** | A and B were relatively comparable, with the former... |

## 三、数据占比表达

```
A, accounting for 18%, is the second reason.
A is the second reason, accounting for 18%.
The second reason is A, taking up 18%.
```

**占比词汇：** account for, take up, make up, constitute

# 📝 PART 3: Task 2 Report

## 一、题型分类

| 类型 | 关键词 | 结构 |
|------|--------|------|
| **Cause & Effect** | reasons, effects, causes | 原因 → 后果 |
| **Problem & Solution** | problems, solutions, measures | 问题 → 解决方案 |
| **Cause & Solution** | Why, measures to tackle | 原因 → 解决方案 |
| **Mixed** | Discuss + opinion | 讨论 → 观点 |

---

## 二、开头段模板

### Cause & Solution / Problem & Solution

```
[改写题目]. This essay will [outline why this has happened / discuss the main problems] and [put forward appropriate solutions / propose some possible solutions].
```

### Cause & Effect

```
[改写题目]. This essay will outline why this has happened and then describe the possible [long-term] effects of the problem.
```

### Mixed Question

```
[改写题目]. There could be several reasons for this, and I consider it to be a [positive/negative] trend.
```

---

## 三、主体段结构

### Problem & Solution

```
主体段1 (Problems):
There are [two main] problems relating to this phenomenon. 
- The first/primary problem is that... [解释] [例子]
- Another problem is that... [解释] [例子]

主体段2 (Solutions):
Despite the severe problems mentioned above, there are solutions to tackle them.
- Firstly, [解决方案]... [解释] [例子]
- Secondly, [解决方案]... [解释] [例子]
```

### Cause & Effect

```
主体段1 (Causes):
There are [several] reasons for this phenomenon.
- The first reason is that... [解释] [例子]
- Another reason is that... [解释] [例子]

主体段2 (Effects):
These factors can lead to several effects.
- Firstly, [后果1]... [解释]
- Secondly, [后果2]... [解释]
```
