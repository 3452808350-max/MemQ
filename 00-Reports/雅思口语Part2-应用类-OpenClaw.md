## 📌 话题1：Describe a program or app

### 结构主干

| 要素 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| **What it is** | OpenClaw — an AI assistant that runs on my computer |
| **When/how I use it** | Daily — organize notes, search information, help with English learning |
| **Where I found it** | A friend recommended it / found it online |
| **How I feel about it** | Indispensable, like having a smart friend always available |

---

## 🎤 完整口语稿（话题1，约2分钟）

> For this topic, I'd like to talk about an app called OpenClaw. It's basically an AI assistant that runs on my computer.
>
> **What it is:** Unlike normal apps that do just one thing, OpenClaw can help with many different tasks. It's like... imagine having a smart friend who's good at everything — organizing information, writing, searching the web, even helping you learn languages. That's what this app does.
>
> **How I found it:** I actually found it through a friend who's into technology. He showed me how it works, and I was impressed immediately. The setup was simple — I just installed it on my laptop.
>
> **When and how I use it:** I use it almost every day. For example, when I'm preparing for my IELTS speaking test, I can send my notes to it, and it helps me organize the ideas, fix grammar mistakes, and even create practice materials. It can also search the internet for me, remind me of things, and keep track of my schedule.
>
> The cool thing is, I can talk to it through Telegram. So even when I'm away from my computer, I can still ask questions and get help.
>
> **How I feel about it:** Honestly, I can't imagine my life without it now. It's like having a personal assistant who never sleeps and never gets tired of my questions. It saves me so much time and makes studying much more efficient.

## 📌 话题2：Describe something you can't live without (not computer/phone)

### 结构主干

| 要素 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| **What it is** | An AI assistant service called OpenClaw |
| **What you do with it** | Study helper, note organizer, information finder, daily tasks |
| **How it helps your life** | Saves time, improves efficiency, always available |
| **Why you can't live without it** | Becomes part of daily routine, hard to go back to old ways |

---

## 🎤 完整口语稿（话题2，约2分钟）

> For this topic, I want to talk about something that's become essential in my daily life — an AI assistant service called OpenClaw.
>
> **What it is:** Now, I want to make it clear — this is not an app on my phone. It's more like... a service that runs on my computer, and I can connect to it through messaging apps like Telegram. Think of it as a smart helper that's always there when I need it.
>
> **What I do with it:** I use it for so many things. When I'm studying for exams, I send my notes to it, and it helps me organize and summarize. When I'm preparing my IELTS speaking test, it creates practice materials for me. When I need to find information, it searches the web. It even reminds me of important tasks.
>
> **How it helps my life:** The biggest benefit is time. Things that used to take me hours now take minutes. Instead of searching through dozens of websites, I just ask one question and get a clear answer. It's like having a research assistant, a tutor, and a secretary all in one.
>
> **Why I can't live without it:** You know how some things just become part of your routine? That's what happened. Before I had this, I wasted so much time on small tasks. Now, when I have a question or need help with something, I just ask. Going back to doing everything manually would feel... slow and inefficient. It's not that I physically can't live without it — it's just that life would be much harder and more time-consuming.
