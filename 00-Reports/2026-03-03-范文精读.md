# 范文精读 - Leadership & Intelligence

> **日期**: 2026-03-03  
> **题目**: Intelligence is the most important factor for a leader. To what extent do you agree or disagree?  
> **题型**: Argument Essay  
> **立场**: Fully Disagree（完全不同意）

---

## 📝 范文（7 分水平）

### It is often argued that intelligence is the most crucial quality that a leader should possess. I completely disagree with this statement. While intelligence is certainly valuable, I believe that integrity and emotional intelligence are far more important for effective leadership.

### Firstly, leaders without integrity can be dangerous regardless of how intelligent they are. History has shown us that many highly intelligent leaders have used their cognitive abilities to manipulate, deceive, or pursue selfish agendas. For example, the CEOs of Enron were undoubtedly brilliant individuals, but their lack of moral principles led to one of the biggest corporate scandals in history. This demonstrates that intelligence without a moral compass can cause catastrophic consequences.

### Furthermore, emotional intelligence matters more than pure intellect when leading teams. A leader's primary role is to inspire, motivate, and understand their followers. Someone with high IQ but low EQ may struggle to build trust, resolve conflicts, or empathize with team members' concerns. Consider Steve Jobs, who was known not for his analytical intelligence but for his ability to understand customer emotions and inspire his team to achieve the impossible.

### Admittedly, intelligence enables leaders to make informed decisions and solve complex problems. However, these cognitive tasks can often be delegated to advisors or experts. What cannot be delegated is the leader's character, their ability to unite people, and their willingness to admit mistakes—qualities that have nothing to do with intelligence.

### In conclusion, while intelligence is a useful attribute for leaders, it is by no means the most important one. Integrity, emotional intelligence, and the ability to inspire others are far more critical for long-term leadership success.

## 💡 可学习句型（5 个）

### 1. 表明立场
```
I completely disagree with this statement.
→ I strongly agree that...
→ I partially agree because...
```

### 2. 提出论点
```
X is far more important for Y.
→ ...is crucial for...
→ ...plays a more critical role than...
```

### 3. 举例论证
```
History has shown us that...
→ A prime example is...
→ This can be seen in the case of...
```

### 4. 对比论证
```
Someone with high X but low Y may struggle to...
→ While X is important, Y matters more when...
```

### 5. 让步反驳
```
Admittedly, X enables... However, ...
→ It is true that... Nevertheless, ...
→ While X is valuable, it is by no means...
```

## ⚠️ 常见错误（避免再犯）

| 错误类型 | 错误示例 | 正确写法 |
|----------|---------|---------|
| **立场不明确** | I partly agree and partly disagree | I completely disagree |
| **段落无主题句** | 直接开始论证 | Firstly, leaders without... |
| **例子无细节** | Many companies failed | The CEOs of Enron... |
| **结论有新观点** | 引入新论点 | 只总结已有论点 |
| **词汇重复** | important...important | crucial...critical |
