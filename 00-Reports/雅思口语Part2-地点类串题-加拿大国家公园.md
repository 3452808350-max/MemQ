## 🎯 核心素材：加拿大 Banff National Park

### 开头引入（通用）
> For this topic, I'd like to talk about Canada, specifically Banff National Park, which is located in the Canadian Rockies in the province of Alberta.

### 怎么知道（通用）
> I first learned about this place from a travel documentary I watched a few years ago. The breathtaking scenery really caught my eye, and I've been dreaming about visiting ever since. Later, a friend of mine who went there confirmed it was even more beautiful in person.

### 景色描述（话题3、4重点）
> It's absolutely stunning there. The park is filled with dense pine forests and towering evergreen trees, some of which are over a hundred years old. When you walk through the trails, sunlight filters through the leaves, creating this magical atmosphere. The air is so crisp and fresh — you can literally smell the pine.
>
> Besides the forests, there are snow-capped mountains, turquoise lakes like Lake Louise, and even glaciers. It's like something out of a postcard.

### 做什么（话题2、4重点）
> I would go hiking on the trails, take photos of the scenery, and sometimes just sit by the lake, enjoying the peace and quiet. It's a perfect place to unwind and recharge.

### 感受（通用）
> What I love most about this place is that it offers a complete escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. No traffic, no notifications — just pure nature. It makes me feel calm and present.

## 📌 话题1：外国短期停留/工作

**题目：** Describe a foreign country you would like to stay or work for a short period of time

| 要素 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| Which country | Canada, Alberta province |
| How you knew | Travel documentary / friend's recommendation |
| What you will do | Work as a seasonal park guide / hospitality; explore nature on weekends |
| Why short period | Working holiday visa (1 year max) / seasonal work opportunity |

**独特部分：**
> If I had the chance, I'd love to work there as a seasonal park guide or in a lakeside hotel during the summer. It'd be an amazing way to experience the local lifestyle while being surrounded by nature.
>
> As for why it's a short period — well, I'm thinking about applying for a working holiday visa, which typically allows you to stay for up to one year. Also, seasonal jobs in national parks are usually temporary, which fits perfectly with my plan to explore and move on to other experiences.

## 📌 话题2：安静的地方

**题目：** Describe a quiet place you like to go

| 要素 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| Where | A quiet corner by Lake Louise / a secluded trail in Banff |
| How you knew | Discovered during my visit / friend showed me |
| How often | Every time I visit, or "if I lived there, I'd go weekly" |
| What you do | Sit quietly, journal, meditate, watch the sunset |
| How you feel | Peaceful, recharged, away from digital noise |

**独特部分：**
> There's this particular spot by Lake Louise, away from the main tourist area, where you can sit on a rock and just... be. No crowds, no noise — just the sound of water and wind through the trees.
>
> If I lived nearby, I imagine I'd go there every weekend. I'd bring a journal, maybe a book, and just disconnect from everything for a few hours. It's the kind of quiet that helps you hear your own thoughts.

## 📌 话题3：有很多树的地方

**题目：** Describe a place with a lot of trees that you would like to visit

| 要素 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| Where | Banff National Park, Canadian Rockies |
| How you knew | Same as above |
| What it's like | Dense pine forests, towering evergreens, 100+ year old trees, sunlight filtering through |
| Why visit | Breathe fresh air, escape urban life, experience pristine nature |

**独特部分：**
> What strikes me most is the sheer density of the forest. You're completely surrounded by trees — pines, spruces, firs — some towering over 30 meters tall. The air quality is incredible too; you can literally breathe in the freshness.
>
> Walking through those trails feels like entering another world. It's humid, cool, and the ground is covered with soft moss. It's the kind of place where you can lose track of time.

## 📌 话题4：自然之地

**题目：** Describe a natural place (e.g. parks, mountains, etc.)

| 要素 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| Where | Banff National Park |
| How you knew | Same as above |
| What it's like | Mountains, turquoise lakes, glaciers, wildlife (elk, bears) |
| Why visit | Hiking, photography, reconnect with nature |

**独特部分：**
> Banff has it all — snow-capped mountains, glacier-fed lakes with that unreal turquoise color, waterfalls, and even wildlife. I've heard you can spot elk, mountain goats, and sometimes even bears from a safe distance.
>
> It's not just a park; it's an ecosystem. Every corner reveals something different — one moment you're by a mirror-like lake, the next you're on a mountain ridge overlooking the valley.
